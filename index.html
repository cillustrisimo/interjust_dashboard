The Universal Justice Project (UJP) is a research project only. Nothing in the UJP or its related analysis should be taken as legal advice. Anyone intending to file a legal action should consult local counsel in the respective jurisdiction for legal advice. The UJP was created with openly available, online, public information. Therefore, there may be information in the UJP that is incorrect because it is outdated, mistranslated, or reflected incorrectly online. We welcome and encourage anyone who has updated information to please contact us: The-Docket@cfj.org.
