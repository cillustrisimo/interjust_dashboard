About the Project

What is Justice Beyond Borders?

The Justice Beyond Borders Project is a comprehensive database tracking the criminalization of the most serious international crimes—war crimes, crimes against humanity, genocide, and aggression—across 216 jurisdictions worldwide.

How to Use This Tool

[Tutorial Video or Interactive Guide]

Methodology

  • Review of 216 jurisdictions (193 UN Member States + 23 other entities)
  • Analysis of criminal codes and statutes
  • Expert review from national practitioners
  • Regular updates as legislation changes

Trends and Challenges

  • Historical development of universal jurisdiction
  • Recent expansion to Eastern Europe and Global South
  • The prosecution gap: 148 can prosecute, only 20 have done so
  • Procedural barriers and political obstacles
  • Geographic imbalance in prosecutions
